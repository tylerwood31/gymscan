# GymScan Brand Reference

## Colors
- Background: #0E0E12
- Surface: #1C1C24
- Primary (amber/gold): #E8A838
- Secondary (teal): #4ECDC4
- Text primary: #F5F5F7
- Text secondary: #8E8E93
- Destructive: #E85454

## Typography
- Headlines: SF Pro Display Bold (-0.5 tracking)
- Body: SF Pro Text Regular
- Numbers/data: SF Pro Rounded Semibold

## Motion
- Default transition: 0.3s ease-in-out
- Equipment list: staggered fade-in (0.05s delay per item)
- Exercise cards: fan-in from bottom
- Rest timer: amber pulse glow

## Voice
- Calm, confident, not hype
- "15 minutes counts" not "no excuses"
- "Maintain what you built" not "push to the limit"
- "We've got your workout handled" not "crush it"
