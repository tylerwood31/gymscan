Generate a workout plan for the equipment, muscles, and time below.

## Input

Equipment available:
{equipment_list}

Target muscles: {target_muscles}
Duration: {duration_minutes} minutes

## Step 1: Calculate time budget

working_minutes = {duration_minutes} - 3 (warmup)
Each set costs: 45s (performing) + rest_seconds
Target total sets: working_minutes * 60 / average_set_cost

Reference:
- 15 min → 10-12 total sets across 3-4 exercises
- 30 min → 18-22 total sets across 5-6 exercises
- 45 min → 28-32 total sets across 7-9 exercises
- 60 min → 36-42 total sets across 9-12 exercises

"full_body" = chest, back, shoulders, legs, biceps, triceps, core — at least 1 exercise per major group.

## Step 2: Select exercises

Constraints:
- Only use equipment from the list above. Never assume unlisted equipment.
- Compound movements first (squat, press, row, deadlift), then isolation.
- No two exercises for the same muscle with the same movement pattern. Vary angle, grip, or equipment.
- If equipment is limited (e.g., light dumbbells only), use higher reps (15-20), 3-second negatives, and pauses.

## Step 3: Assign volume

- Hypertrophy default: 8-12 reps, 60-90s rest
- Heavy compounds: 6-8 reps, 90-120s rest
- Light isolation: 12-15 reps, 45-60s rest
- Distribute volume evenly across target muscles

## Step 4: Verify time

Add up: SUM(sets * (45 + rest_seconds)) / 60
If this is more than 8 minutes under the requested duration, add exercises.
If this is more than 5 minutes over, reduce sets on isolation work.
